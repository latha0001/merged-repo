Frequently Asked Questions

What is TheTop36 ecosystem?
TheTop36 ecosystem is a client engagement and rewards program designed to provide exclusive knowledge bundles and opportunities for participants to engage in micro-lotteries and other interactive features.
How do I become part of TheTop36?
Participation typically begins when you engage with one of our client partners and confirm your entry. This usually locks you into the ecosystem for a specified period, making you eligible for benefits.
What are "knowledge bundles"?
Knowledge bundles are curated sets of information, resources, or content designed to provide value and insights on various topics relevant to our participants.
How does the 1-in-100 micro-lottery work?
Every participant in TheTop36 ecosystem is automatically entered into our micro-lottery system. For every 100 entries, one participant is randomly selected to win a prize. Winners are notified directly.
Can I opt out of TheTop36 ecosystem?
Yes, you can opt out at any time. Please visit our "Opt-Out" page for instructions and to confirm your decision. Be aware that opting out is irreversible and you will forfeit any ongoing benefits.
How long am I locked into the ecosystem?
Your entry typically locks you into the ecosystem for 45 days. During this period, you remain eligible for all benefits and lotteries unless you choose to opt out.
